Effects of coral reef destruction

  1. Decline of tourism sector: in fact coastal areas play a vital role in promoting Zanzibar’s tourism sector which is driven by different factors including the presence of coral reefs. Coral reefs act as a tourist attraction thus, the destruction of the corals will have an effect toward the tourism sector.
  2. Extinction of marine ecosystem: coral reefs acts as keystone species in the marine ecosystem providing habitats for millions of aquatic species. Thus, degradation of coral reefs has resulted into loss of various many fish species in the ocean.
  3. Decline of fishing sector: The destruction of corals will result into degrading the habitats for the aquatic species such as fish thereby declining the fishing activities and the fishing sector in general.
