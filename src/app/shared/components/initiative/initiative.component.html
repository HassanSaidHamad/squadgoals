The use of public disseminated animation videos
This method can be used as a mode of education provision to the local community people. It involves the use of large televisions that will be located along the beach areas. These will be showing animated videos that will be used in imparting the knowledge to the local community people on the ecological importance of coral reefs in the marine ecosystems, causes of coral reef destruction, effects of coral reef destruction and sustainable ways of fishing. It sounds like a geospatial strategy. It is feasible and an easy, quick method that will help to promote awareness and reach such valuable information to a lot of community people. Since these televisions will be located along the shore, then this strategy is directly feasible both in Paje and Tumbatu Island because most of the community people depend much on coastal area for their daily livelihood as they frequently gather at these areas, also each year they conduct their traditional ceremonies for example, “Mvuya” in Tumbatu Island where all the people gather along the shore. Thus, this method would be very feasible and will help much in raising the awareness of the community people concerning the coral reef conservation.
