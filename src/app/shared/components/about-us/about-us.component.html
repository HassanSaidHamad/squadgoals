About-Us

We are “THE SQUAD GOALS” a group of four students from the State University of Zanzibar (SUZA). We are from two different faculty, Geography & Environmental faculty and Information & Technology faculty together joined up in Multi-Competence Learning (MCL) project for the aim of raising various problems facing the coastal communities in Unguja especially in Paje coastal area and Tumbatu Island. Our major focus is on coral reef destruction finding out the local community perspective regarding the corals, causes, effects, and hindrances. With this information, we come up with a geospatial solution to solve the problem in order to enable proper monitoring and conservation of the corals.
